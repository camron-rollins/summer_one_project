Reflection:
Fedrick Doulgass

Quote:“I prefer to be true to myself, even at the hazard of incurring the ridicule of others, rather than to be false, and to incur my own abhorrence.” - Fredrick Douglass

Key words: Allies

Writing:
What the quote is trying to say is that it is better to stay honest to yourself than to change for the better of others that do not care for you or your well being. But they want to change identity as a person. What Fredrick is trying to say is that there is no reason to change you personality identity.
